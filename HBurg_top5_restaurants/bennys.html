Benny Sorrentino's

Benny Sorrentino's is one of those places that you have to try at least once in your life. Benny's is a nationwide chain, but each location has its own twist, which is why it still made our list.

Whether you're just wanting to grab a quick slice (that's the size of a small traffic cone) or wanting to order a full pie (which may or may not be the size of an entire coffee table), Benny Sorrentino's will deliver strong on your craving.

With rotating flavors that are some of the most interesting pizza topping combos you'll ever run into, you will probably start rethinking your go-to pizza order.

If you love pizza, you'll love Benny's.
