Top 5 Restaurants In

Harrisonburg, VA

No matter why you're here, we've got you covered.

Whether you're a student at JMU, a local, or just visiting, this list is for you. Be sure to make time for each place (if you're cool). You won't regret it!

Worried about if you can trust this list or not?

We understand! But don't worry, we thought about that. That's why this list officially has the "stranger guarantee." No, seriously. Ask any student or local if they'd recommend these places. I'll wait.

Billy Jacks

Bar style restaurant opened by two best friends in 2009 with unique unforgettable food with a killer atmosphere. Once you come, you'll want one in your Hometown.

Macado's

Opened in 1978, this is the college town must-have. Good prices, limitless menu options, friendly staff, and an awing atmosphere. What's not to love?

The largest and most unique pizza by the slice. Though not exclusive to Harrisonburg, you have to try it at least once. And will probably want it every time you're in a city with a Benny's.
