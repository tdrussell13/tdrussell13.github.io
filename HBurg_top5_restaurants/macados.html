Macado's

Macado's is THE college town classic. Known for their colossal sandwiches, Macado's takes the art of stacking flavors to new heights. From towering deli delights to mouthwatering wraps, each dish is unique to itself. And that's saying a lot given the size of their menu (just wait until you see it). The menu doesn’t stop at sandwiches though. Burgers, quesadillas, tacos, and much more is offered. Fair warning, you may want to look at the menu before coming in…

Macado's didn't make this list just for their food though. Sports enthusiasts and casual diners alike can lose themselves for hours in the atmosphere of the restaurant. From local centerpieces, to signed jerseys and helmets, the walls and trophy cases in this place will make you think you're in a museum.
