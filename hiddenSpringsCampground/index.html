    Our Campground

    The Hidden Springs Campground has been family owned since it was founded and is located right on the heart of Cheat River at Shaver's Fork. Our campground borders the Monoghalea National Forest and is only 5 miles away from downtown Elkins, WV. We offer 40 campsites, each with water, electric, and sewage hookup for any RV or camper. Amenities include an on-site shower house, a pavilion, and a river for fishing, swimming, tubing, and more! We also have two amazing and affordable vacation rental properties which you can find on both Airbnb and VRBO. Explore what we have to offer and book your stay today!
