    Blackwater Falls State Park

    Blackwater Falls is a must-see spot for visitors. This park offers many hiking trails and amazing views of the 57-foot waterfalls. The trail leading to the falls is 0.25 miles and is made of mostly wooded stairs. This activity is perfect for all ages and is only 39 miles away.

    Durbin & Greenbrier Valley Railroad

    Take a scenic train ride through the wild and wonderful West Virginia at the Durbin & Greenbrier Valley Railroad. There are two riding options ranging from 8 miles to 22 miles, where you and your group can see some of the area's most beautiful sights while enjoying a meal! During the holiday season, the railroad holds a special "Polar Express" event where you can live the story in real life as you embark on a train ride to The North Pole. This is a must-do activity while staying in Elkins!

    Seneca Rocks

    Seneca Rocks is one of the most incredible sights to see in West Virginia. Travelers come from all over just to see this remarkable formation. While at Seneca Rocks, you can visit the Seneca Rocks Discovery Center or even hike up to the observation deck.

    Smoke Hole Caverns

    Smoke Hole Caverns is a breathtaking and interactive walkthrough where you and your group will be guided through the historic caverns and learn all about their rich history. This is a super affordable activity that will awe everyone in your group!

    Explore
    Spruce Knob

    At 4,863 feet above sea level, Spruce Knob is West Virginia's highest peak. This area offers backpacking and day hiking opportunities for the visitors looking to get a splendid view of the gorgeous natural scenery surrounding the area.

    Explore
    Bickle Knob Observation Area

    Bickle Knob Observation Tower is one of our favorite sightseeing activities to do as a family. It is only a short 5-minute walk from the parking lot and offers amazing views of the mountains during sunrise and sunset. This observation area is only 5 miles away.

    Otter Creek Wilderness Area

    The Otter Creek Wilderness is a beautiful natural area spanning over 20,000 acres. This area is filled with tons of streams and creeks and has 45 miles of trails! For a day filled with adventure and nature's most precious moments, definitely check this place out!

    Explore
    High Falls

    The High Falls of Cheat is one of the most beautiful waterfalls in the Monongahela and at an altitude of over 2,800ft, one of the highest. The High Falls of Cheat is horseshoe-shaped, and over 100ft wide, falling 20ft into a large pool below. The High Falls trail passes through old-growth forest, and large hemlock as it winds through the saddle on Shavers Mountain. This is a beautiful yet challenging hike in the backcountry of the Monongahela National Forest.
