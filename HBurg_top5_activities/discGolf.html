Disc Golf

Disc golf is one of those sports that recently took off in popularity during the pandemic. And it was for good reason. Unlike traditional golf, disc golf is inexpensive to try out, requiring only a few discs. And almost every disc golf course offers free admission.

With a similar scoring concept to golf, it is much simpler to understand, and has a much smaller learning curve. It blends elements of strategy, precision, and physical activity, making it a sport suitable for all ages and fitness levels.

Harrisonburg, being the small town it is, even has a few courses of its own. And they’re nice! The two best are JMU’s wooded course, and Westover Park’s course, which combines strategic lightly wooded holes with open field holes. Disc Golf quickly becomes a passion for many people who try it, so you’d be doing yourself a disservice if you don’t give it a shot!
