Pickleball

Pickleball is a fantastic sport for individuals of all ages and skill levels, offering a unique blend of fun and fitness. With a smaller court and slower moving ball than traditional tennis, it’s easy for beginners to pick up and enjoy right away. The accessibility to Tennis and Pickleball courts in Harrisonburg, combined with the ability to play with 4 people, makes it a perfect activity for your family or roommates. What better way to stay active and be around the people you love?

Trying pickleball introduces you to a low-impact, high-energy sport that combines elements of tennis, badminton, and ping pong for an equally fun experience with easier fundamentals. Anyone looking for a new recreational activity should definitely not overlook Pickleball!
