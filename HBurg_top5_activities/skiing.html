Skiing

Skiing is an experience unlike any other. It combines the thrill of speed with the beauty of winter landscapes and brings a large sense of freedom. Whether you ski regularly or have never tried, skiing in Harrisonburg at Massanutten is something we’d be silly to not recommend when in the area.

Skiing is something the whole family can experience. It may take a few times to fully understand proper mobility, but even when you’re learning, you’re having more fun than most any alternative winter activities.

The sense of freedom as you glide down snowy slopes, coupled with the opportunity to enjoy picturesque mountain scenery, makes skiing not only a physically rewarding activity but also an immersive and enjoyable adventure. Go get an adrenaline rush and see those beautiful snowy Shenandoah mountains.
