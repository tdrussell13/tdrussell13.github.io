Nature Walks

Finding some walking trails near you offers most of the benefits that hiking in the area does, but for less of a time commitment and not as much planning. There are many well maintained trails in Harrisonburg that offer great views for everybody. It’s important to exercise and we always recommend everyone gets some outside time to take their mind off of life’s stressors.

Connecting with nature in a non-demanding way is something everyone should do. Who wouldn’t want to breathe crisp air, be surrounded by nature, and see some of the great mountain ranges Harrisonburg has to offer?
